Our Heritage

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors

Zaika represents more than three decades of culinary excellence, rooted in the heart of Islamabad. Founded by Chef Muhammad Ali, our restaurant emerged from a profound passion for preserving and celebrating authentic Pakistani cuisine.

Each dish we prepare tells a story—of bustling bazaars, family gatherings, and time-honored recipes that have been perfected over generations. We believe that fine dining should be an immersive journey through culture, tradition, and exceptional taste.

Our Chef's Team

Chef Muhammad Ali

Executive Chef & Founder

With 35 years of culinary expertise, Chef Muhammad Ali founded Zaika to preserve authentic Pakistani cuisine. His signature dishes have earned recognition across the region.

Chef Hassan Ahmed

Head Chef - Tandoor

Specialized in traditional tandoor cooking, Hassan brings 25 years of expertise in crafting perfectly charred kebabs and naans using ancestral techniques.

Chef Fatima Khan
